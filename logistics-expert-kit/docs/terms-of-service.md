# Terms of Service

`logistics-expert-kit` provides general logistics domain guidance for scope framing, operations diagnosis, metrics design, and risk review.

The output is advisory. It must not be treated as final legal, customs, tax, insurance, safety, contract, rate, SLA, or regulatory advice. Users remain responsible for validating recommendations against their own operating model, contracts, systems, customer commitments, and applicable rules.

The plugin does not perform live carrier lookup, rate comparison, customs classification, route optimization, demand forecasting, or external publishing in v0.1.

By using the plugin, users agree to review any operational recommendation with the relevant business owner before applying it to live logistics operations.

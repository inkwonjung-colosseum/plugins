# Planning Brief

> Draft-only. This is the canonical context and decision brief for the suite.

## Summary

State the planning topic and recommended direction in 2-3 sentences.

## One-Line Problem

State the problem in one clear sentence.

## Current State

Describe what is happening now and why this needs attention.

## Intended Outcome

Describe what should change when the work succeeds.

## Users or Stakeholders

List affected users, stakeholders, decision makers, and reviewers.

## Goals

List outcomes this work should achieve.

## Non-goals

List what this work explicitly will not cover.

## Success Criteria

List observable conditions that mean the work succeeded.

## Failure Criteria

List observable conditions that mean the work failed or should not proceed.

## Constraints

List timeline, dependency, policy, resource, or technical constraints.

## Options Considered

Compare 2-3 viable directions when a real choice exists. State if no alternative is currently under consideration.

## Recommended Direction

Describe the recommended direction and why it is preferred.

## Risks

Use the risk table snippet when risk tracking is needed.

## Assumptions

List unsupported or unverified statements here.

## Evidence and Sources

Link source notes, tickets, documents, research, or meeting records.

## Open Questions

List questions that must be answered before deeper planning, review, or handoff.
